Customer Engagement Strategy for Astra Security's Pentest Offerings

Astra Security's customer engagement strategy is built around its core Pentest offerings and strong support systems, with a focus on proactive communication and delivering continuous value at every stage of the customer journey.

Positioned as a "Next-generation Penetration Testing Platform," Astra is shifting away from one-off security checks to offer continuous, integrated protection, aligned with today's DevSecOps needs.

The platform supports a wide range of pentests, from Web and Mobile Apps to APIs, Cloud, Network, and even Blockchain, making it highly versatile for businesses across industries.

Customer Engagement Touchpoints

Pre-Sales / Discovery

The CSM collaborates with the sales team pre-handoff to understand the customer's security needs, business goals, and technical setup. This ensures a seamless transition post-sale.

Onboarding

A dedicated CSM and a shared Slack channel are introduced. A kickoff call aligns scope and expectations, guiding the customer through Astra's 7-step pentest process.

Adoption & Value Realization

CSM tracks platform usage, reviews vulnerabilities, highlights fixes, and promotes features like AI-generated fix recommendations and compliance dashboards.

Expansion & Advocacy

During QBRs, CSMs highlight security improvements and identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities. Customers are encouraged to use Astra's Publicly Verifiable Pentest Certificate.

Strategy for High Customer Renewal Rates

Astra's renewal success hinges on demonstrating continuous, visible, and quantifiable value to customers. The CSM plays a crucial role in translating Astra's technical features into clear business outcomes.

Proactive Communication & Relationship Management

  • Regular touchpoints (monthly/quarterly check-ins).
  • Use shared Slack channels for faster collaboration.
  • Conduct QBRs/EBRs to highlight vulnerabilities fixed, compliance progress, and ROI.

Reinforce & Demonstrate Ongoing Value

  • Emphasize continuous pentesting and CI/CD integration.
  • Share metrics and success stories.
  • Promote Public pentest certificate, Compliance enablement, and Zero false positives.

Customer Health Monitoring & Risk Mitigation

  • Implement health scores using usage data.
  • Identify at-risk accounts early and intervene with extra support.
  • Collect feedback via NPS/CSAT surveys.

Loyalty, Education & Upselling

  • Provide ongoing product training (webinars, guides).
  • Foster community building.
  • Identify opportunities for upsell/cross-sell.

Sample Customer Response: Pentest Quality & Delayed Delivery

Call the SPOC on the customer side to take responsibility and accountability for the current situation. Acknowledge that the complaint has been registered, ask questions to understand the exact nature of the problem, make a note of the details, and communicate them to the relevant internal teams.

Subject: Let's Make This Right - Astra Security

Hello <Customer Name>!

I'm really sorry to hear about your experience with the delayed delivery and quality of the Pentest. I completely understand how frustrating that must be, and I want you to know we're taking this seriously.

Here's what we're doing right away:
  • A senior security engineer is reviewing your Pentest report, and we'll share an updated, verified version as soon as possible. <We can promise a timeline for turnaround as confirmed by the relevant technical team>
  • Your case has been prioritized in our queue to make sure there are no further delays.

Going forward, we're adding extra quality checks for your account and will keep you updated regularly so you're never left in the dark again.

I'd also love to set up a quick call with our technical team once the updated report is ready - just to walk you through it and hear your thoughts. Looking forward to catching up with you soon!

Thank you for your patience.

Note: Constant communication ensures the customer feels valued. A gesture of goodwill (e.g., free re-scan, extended support) is a best practice.
